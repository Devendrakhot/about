About Chinmaya Prakashan: Bringing Knowledge to Light

Welcome to the official online home of Chinmaya Prakashan, the publishing arm of the worldwide spiritual and educational movement, Chinmaya Mission.

Story & Vision

Guided by the inspiration of Swami Chinmayananda, Chinmaya Prakashan holds a single, powerful vision: to bring the wisdom of Vedanta to the modern audience. Since its inception, the publishing house has been dedicated to translating ancient wisdom into expressions that resonate with today's seeker.

This knowledge is regarded as the key to inner transformation. By deepening self-understanding, life becomes more purposeful and peaceful, enabling positive contributions to family, workplace and society at large. With over 900 titles in multiple languages, the mission remains clear: helping every reader discover connection with the true Self.

Offerings: Resources for Every Seeker

From the quiet of solitude to the hustle and bustle of everyday life, Chinmaya Prakashan presents a refreshing array of resources to meet diverse needs of every seeker:

  • For Householders: Practical insights for navigating worldly matters with wisdom.
  • For Professionals & Corporates: Guidance to harmonize work-life balance and infuse action with meaning.
  • For Children: Engaging stories, prayers and books to nurture love, learning and growth.
  • For Youth: Direction to channel immense potential and cultivate a strong value system.
  • For Spiritual Seekers: Deeper wisdom for devotion, reflection and enquiry.

Truly, there is something for everyone.

Formats

To suit different lifestyles and preferences, Chinmaya Prakashan shares wisdom in multiple formats:

  • Physical Books: Hardcovers and paperbacks for personal libraries.
  • E-Books: Digital publications for instant access on any device.
  • Audio Content: Pen drives, Audibles and preloaded sound boxes for learning on the go.
  • Video Content: Pen drives with discourses and talks for an immersive experience.

Reach

Resources are made available through multiple channels:

  • Retail Stores: Chinmaya Vani outlets located across India and worldwide.
  • Online Platforms: A digital presence on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, blogs and other platforms.
  • Chinmaya Vani e-Shop: A single destination ensuring direct access to publications with just a click.
  • Collaborations: Strategic partnerships with established Publishing Houses and Distribution Centres for wider accessibility.
